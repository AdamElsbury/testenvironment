Some conditions affect visual test results...

Exemptions

Please do not complete the test if any of the following criteria apply to you. This is nothing personal but each condition will give false/manipulated readings.

  • Clinically blind
  • Schizophrenia
  • Auditory or visual hallucinations
  • Dementia with Lewy Bodies

If you do have any of the above conditions and would like to be included in further dissertation research please get in touch with me!
