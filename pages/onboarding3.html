How will the test data be stored and is it safe?

Data protection & Ethics

The data being collected is strictly related to what element on each web page is clicked. No personal information is being collected or stored. No cookies or login information is being stored. Each test submission is completely anonymous.

This is part of a dissertation research study so the results will be published in the dissertation and on a single webpage being used as a dissertation project show in Southampton Solent University. The data will be stored automatically using web services and input into a password protected database. All data will be controlled and stored solely by the primary researcher (Adam Elsbury).

By clicking proceed, I confirm that I am happy to share my test results with the primary researcher, any official member of the University marking board and those present at the dissertation show in Southampton Solent University.
